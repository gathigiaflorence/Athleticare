# AthletiCare 

AthletiCare is an Android-based mobile application developed to improve athlete healthcare management within sports institutions and teams. The system connects coaches and physiotherapists through a centralized platform where athlete injuries, recovery progress, appointments, and recommendations can be managed efficiently.

The main goal of AthletiCare is to simplify communication between sports staff while ensuring athletes receive proper care, timely treatment, and structured recovery monitoring.

---

##Problem Statement

In many sports environments, athlete injury records, treatment updates, and communication between coaches and physiotherapists are often handled manually. This can lead to:

- Poor injury tracking  
- Delayed treatment follow-ups  
- Miscommunication between coaches and medical staff  
- Lack of organized athlete records  
- Difficulty monitoring athlete recovery progress  

AthletiCare solves these challenges through a digital management system.

## Key Features

##Coach Dashboard

Coaches can:
- View athletes of their team  
- Add new athletes to the team database  
- View physiotherapist recommendations  
- Monitor injury and recovery updates    

## Physiotherapist Dashboard

Physiotherapists can:

- Manage athlete appointments  
- Record injuries and assessments  
- Create follow-up sessions  
- Add treatment plans  
- Update recovery status  
- Share recommendations with coaches  

## Athlete Management

- Register athlete profiles  
- Store athlete details securely  
- Organize athletes by team or coach  
- Maintain athlete health records  

## Other Medics

- Generate team reports  
- View athlete injury summaries

## Why AthletiCare?

AthletiCare creates a more professional sports support system by combining healthcare and team management in one platform.

Benefits include:

- Faster communication  
- Better athlete care  
- Organized medical records  
- Improved injury monitoring  
- Efficient appointment scheduling  
- Better return-to-play decisions
